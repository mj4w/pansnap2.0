Pansnap Studio Terms & Conditions

The following Terms & Conditions are between Pansnap Studio (Ms. Franzin Bautista & Mr. Jhomer Valiente) and the Client/s who will hire the studio as their photographer and/or videographer. These Terms & Conditions are also bound to the Client’s Guests/Family/Attendees during the shoot. It is the Client’s responsibility to educate and/or distribute the said Terms & Conditions to all person(s) involved in the booked session.
This agreement will become effective and binding, once the reservation or the Client has been confirmed and the payment and/or reservation fee has been received by Pansnap Studio.
1. Reservation and Payment Terms
a. The reservation of the Client/s is subject to approval. The Client should wait for a call within the day to check if the details/date that was selected is available.
b. Once the reservation has been approved, the client is required to pay a reservation fee not lower than Php 500 for studio shoot services and not lower than 50% of the total amount for outdoor shoot services.
c. The reservation fee is payable through Gcash or in the Studio within 2 days to secure the date and time agreed on. Failing to pay within the duration will remove the Client’s reserved date.
d. Full payment of the Client’s service balance should be done after the scheduled shoot
2. Cancellation
a. At the time the reservation takes effect, Pansnap Studio shall reserve the date and time agreed upon. If the client cancels the appointment for any reason, all of the payments are non-refundable to offset its loss of business. However, the Client can reschedule the appointment for 1 chance only.
During the Shoot
a. The Client understands that appointment times are reserved according to a shooting schedule. In the case of the Client not being on time, Pansnap Studio will not be held responsible for the loss of suitable shooting time or the missed amount of images and/or videos agreed upon not obtained within the time remaining. However, the Pansnap Studio team can adjust the time set by the Client if there is a possible chance.
b. For studio shoots, the Client/s may bring companions/assistants, but make sure to limit the number since the studio can accommodate a small number of people per appointment.
4. Weather Condition
a. Pansnap Studio cannot control the weather. All outdoor shoots are dependent on weather conditions.
b. The Client will not force the Photographer and/or Videographer to use their equipment if the weather is not suitable for the condition. In case of unsuitable weather conditions, the shoot may stop and the date will be rescheduled on an agreed date as soon as possible.
Editing Time Duration
a. After the shoot, the editing time of photos will take a minimum of 1 – 2 weeks for studio shoots while 2 – 3 weeks to a maximum of 1 month for outdoor shoots
b. Video editing will consume a minimum of 2 – 3 weeks to a maximum of 1 and a half months.
6. Original Files
a. All images and/or video clips captured by Photographer and Videographer are in RAW format to ensure high-quality photos and videos. No RAW files will be given to the Client under any circumstances. These files are large in size and require specialized software to view and edit.
7. Revisions
a. If the Client is not satisfied with the output/s, they may request a revision for 1 chance only to meet their desired look on the output/s.
8. Photo and/or Video Copies
a. By default, photos and/or videos will be sent to the Facebook Account of the Client through a Google Drive folder. It will be only available for 3 weeks. The Client should ensure to download a copy of files within the said duration. After the 3 weeks duration, files inside the Google Drive folder will be automatically deleted without prior notice.
b. The Client can have a copy of files through a flash drive by availing it in the reservation form under the additionals/extras.
c. Print products typically take an additional 1 to 4 weeks to finish and the Client will be contacted to collect them as soon as it's ready.
9. Social Media Posting
a. Pansnap Studio will ask permission from the Client before posting their photos and/or videos on the Facebook Page of studio.
10. Entire Agreement
a. These Terms & Conditions contain the entire understanding between Pansnap Studio and Client/s. It supersedes all prior agreements between the parties. The only way to add or change this agreement is to do so in writing, signed by all the parties.
